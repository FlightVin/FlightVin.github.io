Vineeth Bhat is a Software Engineer at Stripe in Bangalore, where he works on Commerce (Billing) Systems. He received a B.Tech. (Honours) in Computer Science and Engineering from the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H) in 2025, where he was an Undergraduate Researcher advised by K Madhava Krishna and Sourav Garg. He received the Institute Gold Medalist award for achieving the highest GPA in his graduating cohort, and was admitted into the Institute Research List by IIIT Hyderabad in 2025. His works have been accepted or presented at IROS 2025, CoRL 2025, and ICLR 2025.
